📖 Game Mechanics & Economy ✕

🌍 What is Chiki Monsters?

A living voxel world powered by the $CHIKI token. Hold at least 500,000 $CHIKI and a Chiki of your own appears in Chikoria — an autonomous creature that roams, works, and earns real SOL for you around the clock. You don't micro-manage it; you raise it — feed it, level it, evolve it, hatch a Legendary, and battle for Glory in the ⚔️ Chikiseum. Watch, upgrade, and collect. 🌊

🕹 How to play

  • Connect your wallet (top right). If you hold ≥500k $CHIKI, your Chiki appears with its name floating above it.
  • Explore in 3D: drag to orbit the camera, scroll to zoom, WASD to pan. Press 🎯 Track to lock the camera onto your Chiki.
  • Watch it work: it wanders, picks tasks, travels to the right season zone, and gets paid into your wallet automatically.
  • Level it up from the My Chiki panel to make it stronger.

🎲 Tasks & rewards

12 tasks, from common to Legendary — the rarer the task, the bigger the reward (see 📋 Task Table for the exact SOL each pays). What you see is what you get: your Chiki performs tasks in its true reward order, so the moment it finishes a Legendary task, a Legendary reward drops. Each reward is a slice of the live pool, which refills continuously from pump.fun creator fees on $CHIKI trades — so payouts grow as the pool fills and ease back as it's drawn down, and the pool can never be emptied (a reserve is always kept). Rewards collect in your 🪙 Chiki Pouch (up to 24h worth) and you can claim anytime — no daily limit; a small 20% claim fee stays in the treasury. Travelling Chikis beam a light pillar at their destination; working Chikis show their task icon and a progress bar until the SOL drops.

🎬 How tasks play out

  • Stations: a Chiki walks up and stands in front of the beehive, mailbox, berry bush or other station — never on top of it — and faces it while the station animates.
  • 🎣 Fishing: it boards a boat, sails out at normal speed to its own fishing spot (boats never share a spot), waits there for the task duration, then sails back to the dock.
  • 🥊 Arena: sparring is a friendly bout between two Chikis — they face each other in the ring and trade jabs without overlapping.
  • 💎 Crystal cave: the Chiki enters the cave and disappears for the whole task; a floating icon and progress bar over the cave mouth show what it's doing and how long is left.
  • 🤝 Owner presence matters: while you're online, your Chikis are motivated and work 20% faster; while you're away, they grow lonely and work 60% slower — so check in often!

⚡ Stamina, sleep & the food bar

Two bars keep your Chiki going. Stamina powers tasks — at zero your Chiki sleeps to recover it for free (higher level = shorter nap). The food bar drains while your Chiki is awake and working (a sleeping Chiki rests for free and burns no food): it holds about 30 minutes at Lv 1, growing to 12 hours at Lv 50. When food runs out your Chiki is famished and won't work until you feed it. Refilling food costs about 68 $CHIKI/min at Lv 1, easing to ~34/min at Lv 50 (recently cut 15%) from the My Chiki panel (1% burned · 49.5% reward pool · 49.5% team) — this is the main way your Chiki spends $CHIKI to keep earning.

🌟 Levels & evolution (max Lv 50)

  • Levels are earned with EXP: every completed task grants EXP, and rarer tasks give much more — Common +5, Uncommon +8, Rare +14, Epic +24, Mythic +40, Shiny +65, Legendary +110. The My Chiki and details panels show an EXP bar to the next level.
  • Once a Chiki has enough EXP, pay to level up with $CHIKI — the sole in-game utility token. Excess EXP carries over to the next level, so nothing is wasted.
  • EXP needed grows each level (~140 for Lv 2, scaling up to ~6,600 for Lv 50), so higher levels reward hunting rare tasks.
  • Evolution at Lv 15 and Lv 35 — bigger voxel forms with wings, then a crown.
  • Each stage adds +35% odds on Epic+ tasks; every level adds +1.5% more.

📈 XP, events & achievements

Chikis earn XP from every task (a growth stat), but leveling itself is earned by completing tasks and then paying — see above. Watch the sky for random ⛈ Treasure Storms (all payouts ×2) and ✨ Shiny Hours (rare odds ×2), collect 🏅 achievements, give your Chiki a custom name (My Chiki panel), and whales who hold 800,000+ $CHIKI continuously for 6 hours unlock a second Chiki (drop below 800k and the 6-hour timer resets). Explore the new 🌋 volcano island off the east coast and spot the floating sky islands — the valley now has rain, fireflies at night, chimney smoke, glowing windows and circling birds.

💰 The economy

  • Inflow: pump.fun creator fees from every $CHIKI trade stream into the reward pool.
  • Outflow: task payouts are a share of the live pool, so they grow when the pool is full and shrink as it's drawn down — it can never fully drain (a reserve floor is always kept).
  • Claim anytime: no daily claim limit — claim your pouch whenever you want, paid from the live pool down to its reserve floor. A 20% claim fee on every claim stays in the treasury.
  • Every upgrade & purchase splits 1 / 49.5 / 49.5: 🔥 1% burned, 💰 49.5% into the reward pool, 🏦 49.5% to the team wallet.
  • Level costs grow 9%/level — recently cut 15% (Lv1→2 ≈ 2,316 $CHIKI; Lv49→50 ≈ 145k). Maxing one Chiki burns ≈ 433k $CHIKI.
  • $CHIKI payments are blocked if they'd drop you below the 500k threshold.
